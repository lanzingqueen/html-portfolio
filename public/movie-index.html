Comfort Movies to Rewatch

Movies for a Rainy Day or to Cheer Me Up

The Princess Bride

She gets kidnapped, he gets killed, but it all ends up okay. This gem has everything: fencing, fighting, torture, revenge, giants, monsters, chases, escapes, true love, miracles...

The Sound of Music

I watched this one every day, multiple times a day when I was three years old. It just makes me feel safe and cozy. It may not be for everyone, but it's there for me.

Some Like It Hot

This black and white gem features Jack Lemmon, Marilyn Monroe, and Tony Curtis in a hilarious trek where the guys dress in drag to evade gangsters and travel to Florida with an all-girls band. It also has one of the best last lines of any movie ever.

When Harry Met Sally

This one gets me because who doesn't want to fall in love with their best friend? With comedy, drama, and romance, this one tugs at the heartstrings while making you laugh.
